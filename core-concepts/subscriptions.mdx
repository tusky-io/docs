Tusky offers three plans. All plans include the same core features — the difference is scale. Walrus storage costs (WAL) are always billed separately from your [balance](/core-concepts/balance) regardless of plan.

## Plans

| | **Pay As You Go** | **Developer** | **Business** |
| --- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Monthly price** | $0 | $50 | $500 |
| **Storage included** | 5 GB | 100 GB | 2 TB |
| **Transactions / month** | 10,000 | 100,000 | 1,000,000 |
| **Bandwidth / month** | 50 GB | 500 GB | 10 TB |
| **Aggregators** | 1 (shared) | 1 (private) | 3 (private) |
| **Custom domains** | — | — | ✓ |

## Overage behavior

When you exceed a monthly limit:

| Limit exceeded | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| **Storage** | New uploads are blocked until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade. |
| **Transactions** | Requests return `429 Too Many Requests` until the next cycle or until you upgrade. |
| **Bandwidth** | Downloads through your aggregator are throttled. Files remain accessible — just slower. |
