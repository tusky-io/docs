For organizations with significant storage needs, Tusky offers **enterprise plans** with volume-based pricing, dedicated support, and custom SLAs. Enterprise plans are designed for teams pushing terabytes of data through the Walrus network.

## What's Included

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Volume Discounts" icon="tags">
    Lower per-GB rates than pay-as-you-go pricing. The more you store, the less you pay per unit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dedicated Support" icon="headset">
    Priority access to the Tusky engineering team via a private Slack or Discord channel and email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom SLAs" icon="file-contract">
    Guaranteed uptime, response times, and throughput tailored to your requirements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Priority Infrastructure" icon="server">
    Dedicated publisher and aggregator capacity to ensure consistent performance under load.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Enterprise vs. Standard Pricing

| Feature | Pay-As-You-Go | Enterprise |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Tusky fee** | 10% of Walrus cost | Custom (lower) |
| **Support** | Community / email | Dedicated channel + priority response |
| **SLA** | Best effort | Contractual uptime guarantee |
| **Aggregator** | Shared CDN infrastructure | Priority capacity, custom WAF rules |
| **Analytics retention** | 90 days | 1 year+ |
| **Onboarding** | Self-serve | White-glove setup assistance |

## Use Cases

Enterprise plans are ideal for:

- **Media platforms** storing and delivering large video and image libraries.
- **Data archival** services migrating petabytes from centralized storage to Walrus.
- **Web3 applications** that need reliable, high-throughput decentralized storage.
- **Compliance-sensitive organizations** requiring contractual SLAs and audit trails.
